A Billion Eyes

A Billion Eyes is a three-piece band from the Cologne/Bonn area, formed in 2016. Oliver "Ollo" Robinson on guitar, Ralf "Romantic" Nöbel on drums, and Tobias "Emp" Empacher on vocals and bass guitar play pure rock with punk and metal influences.
