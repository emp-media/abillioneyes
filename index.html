A Billion Eyes

Pure rock from Cologne/Bonn

A Billion Eyes is a three-piece band from the Cologne/Bonn area in Germany, formed in 2016. Oliver "Ollo" Robinson on guitar, Ralf Nöbel on drums, and Tobias "Emp" Empacher on vocals and bass play pure rock with punk and metal influences.
